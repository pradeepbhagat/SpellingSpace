📱 Want custom-typed words to sound natural too? (one-time, free)
  1. Open Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices → English.
  2. Tap a voice, then download one marked (Enhanced) or (Premium) — e.g. Ava (Premium) or Samantha (Enhanced).
  3. Come back here, reload the page, and pick the ⭐ voice above.
Only affects words you type that aren't pre-recorded. The class words and letters already use the built-in natural voice.
